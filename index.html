About Me关于我

I am Aoyu Liu, an undergraduate student at the Yingcai Honors College, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, where I am pursuing a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science.

我是刘翱瑜，电子科技大学英才学院、 电子科技大学本科生，攻读数理基础科学学士学位。
